Plot-line:
General Qin Shi Tao was very famous because of his deadliest operations against his enemies. Evil Juhzizz, who tried to spread chaos in Europe, was payed for his plan a very big price. GLA was fully annihilated, and Europe became peaceful. But the time of peace and violence absence was not long. 
Year after, American military scientists explored strange islands in the Pacific sector. They found ruins of the ancient civilization, which was very poweful before the humans ages. Scientists explored, that Ningen Empire had high technologic level and controlled big part of the ancient Pacific sector. Origins of this civilization was not identified. General Alexis Alexander made a theory, that Ningens came to our Earth from the parallel world (alternative quantum reality). 
Then all research bases of USA lost contact. General Leang asked Tao and his forces to check the situation. What was the reason of the stopped connection? And why all military bases in Pacific became silent?
During the exploration of islands, Tao became to understand: it was very bad idea to learn Ningen technologies. The ancient evil has been returned. And now the whole mankind is in heavy danger.

Installation:
Put all maps to the Maps folder (in Documents and Settings/User/Generals Zero Hour Data).
Activate it from the skirmish game. 
At first you need play Mission 1, then Mission 2 and at last Mission 3.
The Bonus mission you need to play only after the full walkthrough of the campaign.

This map pack is strictly for original game. It doesn`t compatible with any mods because of special INI system.
