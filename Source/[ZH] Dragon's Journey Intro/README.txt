Dragon's Journey Intro
Created by VeLoXiuS
This is a single player mission map

On this mission you will get a hacker that was sended by a UN humvee, you will need to protect him until he gets data from the radio tower. You will lose instantly if the hacker dies. You will play the mission without any dozer and Comand Center to build and you cannot capture any building. the chinese spy building in the front of your base will give you a radar (if garrisoned) and the oil factory at the back will give you the ability to own most of the Oil derricks (if garrisoned). Only 2 barracks, 2 warfactory, 2 nuclear reactors are given to you and you get your cash only from the Oil derricks.

Main objective is to escort the UN hacker to the designated radio tower and don't let him get killed. There's also timer until the data from the radio tower get deleted, so you must escort the hacker before the timer ends or you lose and do not destroy the radio tower
