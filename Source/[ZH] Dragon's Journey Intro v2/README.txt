Dragon's Journey 00 - Battle of The Dragons V2.0

Fixed version from the previous map

The First campaign of Dragon's Journey. In this map you will face General Tao as your opponent. Tao has 3 bases, one of them is on your left side of the map then one of them is on your south side of the map and his main base at the southwest of your map.

You're given access to use all china vanilla technology as well as it's superpower. Your base is located at the upper right corner of the map with 2 supply docs and 2 oil derricks, you have 3 gattling cannons at the left side hill and 2 power plant available from the start.

Your mission are :
1.Destroy General Tao's entire base
2.Destroy All spy outpost that was scattered around this area (it's shown on your radar event)
3.Capture General Tao's experimental nuclear reactor (the location already revealed on the map and it's inside Tao' main base)
4.Capture ONE of General Tao's Command Center
5.Arrest General Tao.
6.Don't Lose your main Command Center (or the game ends)

Tao has 5 nukes from the start and some of his nukes will active after a few minutes or after you enter a trigger area that activates it. 2 of his early nuke won't fire at your base, it will destroy an uncaptured tech building at the top middle of the map. one of them will target an oil farm and the other one will target a strategy center (it will give you a surprise).

if you manage to destroy Tao base on the south or on the left (includes all building except base defenses) if it still had some base defense left, then it will receive power from the main base. Tao base on the left and south will active after few minute's and his main base will active after 20 minutes or if a trigger gets activated. Some of his units are fully upgraded so be ready for it.
