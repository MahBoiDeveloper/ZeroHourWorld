----------------------------------------
Chinese Air Defense Network, 2023 rework
----------------------------------------

A semi-stealth commando mission where you must sneak behind enemy lines, link-up with a base that we've lost contact with, and destroy the air defenses.


----
Lore
----

General, we are about to finalize our take-over of China with an attack on Beijing. One obstacle remains in our way, an air defense line.
We could possibly succeed without our air assets, but casualties will be staggering.

To prevent those casualties, you are being sent to clear our the air defense network.

A team of commandos will be dropped behind enemy lines. You will use them to link up with our secret base, and destroy the air defenses.

We have a tank battalion that was able to flank from the north-west and should be able to help you in your mission if you link up with them.


Keep an eye out for secrets, and good luck, General.

- Yuri's Director, CnCNet.


----------------
Troubleshooting:
----------------

- If the next scripts don't fire after the tank attack then some may have gotten stuck near the airbase
- If the intro cinematic camera is messed up then press "Home"
  on your keyboard to reset the gentool camera pitch, then restart the mission.
  Also make sure your camera height is set to default, but this shouldn't affect things during cinematics.

ShowWholeMap.zip

Can be used as an addon in GenLauncher.
