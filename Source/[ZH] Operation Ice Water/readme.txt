
*MAP NAME* By ViTeXFTW

GAME INFO:
This map uses timers to create sudo random events. It is therefore advised to play the map at around 35 game speed plus minus 5.

STORY:
General Tao and General Fai are defending a vital part of the mountains. You mission is to destroy their bases and grant us control over this area.
You arsenal is limited in the beginning but reinforcements will join you during your attempt to gain control. Good luck General.
