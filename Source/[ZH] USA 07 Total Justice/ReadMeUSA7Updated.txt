Briefing :

General

After what happened at the China-Kazakhstan border, Beijing was shocked to see one of their own generals attacking our forces and have declared
Chan Wei & his army as "rogue". Even we have destroyed his base, he managed to escape after our forces crossed the border. The Chinese 
authorities have given us a file describing him and also the reason he has betrayed his country and his allies :

Chan Wei was a officer of General Tsing Shi Tao, a high ranking general directing the nuclear branch of the Chinese army. During the 
First War against the GLA, he illustrated himself by claming important victory for his country. His victories allowed him to get promoted
to General of Class AAA and to have his personal army : the 2nd Nuclear Division.He was reported to be very unstable & reckless in his actions including extremes
uses of nuclear weapons and also be too much patriotic for his country. For example, he refused to get help from US forces to get rid of 
a GLA stronghold. No incidents was reported after the end of the First War against the GLA but everything restarted after we destroyed 
the GLA in Louisiana.

Apparently, he didn't like to see the USA back to the international stage. So, he decided to work with the GLA indirectly to avoid our return :
He first decided to give a Nuclear Missile Silo to the GLA in Morocco. After learning the GLA's failure, he decided to intervene in
Crete to get rid of the GLA and of us at the same time but we were faster than him before he execute his plan. Knowing that he wasn't able
destroy us directly, he gave the GLA the plans and the location of the Western Chinese Main Base to make sure to discredit our forces to
defend the base but our fast response did not allow him to make his plan. Seeing that his "GLA Allies" were not able to make his work,
he finally decide to make a false call for help to trap us but as we know, he escaped just before we neutralize his base.

After days of research, we have finally found him : he has regrouped his best elements of his army to launch a reign of terror in 
Hong-Kong. Hong-Kong did not fully recovered from the First War events : the Tsing Ma bridge was only rebuilt that Chan Wei destroyed it 
again to avoid Chinese authorities to launch a counterattack, the Convention Center Area was converted to one of the 2nd Nuclear Division
base and the Wan Chai Sports Ground has been destroyed and a Training camp of the 2nd Nuclear Division was built here. 

We are going to launch a full attack on a defended beach sector by sea
Our forces will be covered by battleships and by the USS Daedalus. Then, we will build a base to launch attacks on Chan Wei and his
2nd Nuclear Division. We need to be careful as he's going to use nuclear weapons to destroy us ! 

This victory is very crucial and will determine the fate of the free world. 

Good luck, general !

Notes :

- Beijing has given us authorization to use any Chinese tech for our mission
- We will have support of two battleships and the USS Daedalus !
