Dragon's Journey 02 - Road of Despair

We have managed to capture General Tao and defeat the GLA resistance with the help of General Kwai and now we're escorting General Tao POW truck to the place where his trial will be held. General Kwai gives us his heavy tank to escort the POW truck.

Little do we know about the GLA resistance. They've actually had some survivor running for their lives from the previous battle and now they're re-grouping  on one of their hidden outpost and seeking for revenge.

Mission Objective :
Escort The POW truck to it's destionation on the other side of the map

NOTE :
The POW truck will move once you eliminate the thread and it will stop once it gets close enought to the ambush area or is it ?

There will be 4 difficulty for this map. 
1. Easy : If you just want to have fun this is the most reccomended difficulty to play
2. Medium : Almost the same as easy difficulty but the ai has more upgrade and some unit for the ai got replaced
3. Hard : More demo traps and more upgrade for the ambush
4. Brutal : Reduce the amount of Emperor you have and you won't get any ECM from the reinforcement in exchange you get more ECM and battlemaster early on, there's also some unit changes for the AI and lastly there's a timer so you can feel the pressure. When the timer ends you'll know why it's called Brutal.

Tips : if you're having a hard time passing through the top bridge, the answer is to bring your ECM and disable one of the GLA artilery and bring your emperor close to the bridge while clearing the mines (force firing the ground for those who don't know how to do it select a unit then hold ctrl and click anywhere you want it to shoot)

Clue to pass the forest on the left (only for hard and brutal): they're hiding behind those rocks
