Dragon's Journey 01 - Chasing The Dragon's Tail

General Tao has manage to escape to the west, close enought to the border of the middle east. He doesn't have any fund so he sells his nuclear technology to the GLA Resistance in that area.

2 weeks later General Tao and the GLA resistance attacked one of General Kwai outpost. Apparently the deal that he made with the GLA requires him to help the GLA to eliminate General Kwai outpost in that area.

Mission objective :
1. Destroy General Tao new base accross the bridge
2. Eliminate all GLA in this area
3. Defend General Kwai base
4. Defend the Ground Control Center
5. Arrest General Tao and seize his Nuclear technology from the GLA

There's 2 way to complete the mission
1. Destroy Tao base and take over his economy and then destroy the GLA
2. Defend your base from Tao and GLA attack while supporting General Kwai with air support and then he'll clear the GLA with his tanks
